My favourite shark

My first favourite shark are Thresher sharks.

Three Fun Facts About Thresher Sharks!

  1. Thresher sharks are able to jump 20 feet up in the air
  2. They were named after foxes
  3. Their tails can grow up to 5.7 meters long

Three Fun Facts About Whale Sharks

  1. Whale sharks are sharks not whales
