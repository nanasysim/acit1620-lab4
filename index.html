My favourite shark

My first favourite shark are Thresher sharks.

Thresher sharks are one of my favourites because of their long tail and their ability to jump out of the water and into the air.

Three Fun Facts About Thresher Sharks!

  1. Thresher sharks are able to jump 20 feet up in the air
  2. They were named after foxes
  3. Their tails can grow up to 5.7 meters long

My second favourite shark

My second favourte shark are Whale sharks.

I like whale sharks because they are similar to actual whales.

Three Fun Facts About Whale Sharks

  1. Whale sharks are sharks not whales
  2. Whale sharks are filter feeders
  3. They even have teeth in their eyes
